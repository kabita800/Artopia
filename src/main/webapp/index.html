In The Spotlight

Priya Sharma — Artist of the Month

Priya Sharma
Oil Painter

Priya Sharma is a Kathmandu-based oil painter whose work explores the interplay between Himalayan light and traditional Nepali cultural motifs. With over a decade of practice and exhibitions in Nepal, India, and South Korea, she is one of Artopia's founding artists.

Her paintings are held in private collections across the region and have been featured in several national art publications.

Our Story

Connecting Art &
Collectors

Artopia was born from a simple idea: every artist deserves a platform, and every art lover deserves access to original work.

🎨

Our Mission

To create a secure and easy-to-use online platform where art can be presented and sold to more people across Nepal and beyond.

🤝

Our Vision

A thriving creative economy in Nepal where talented artists can earn a sustainable livelihood from their passion.

⭐

Our Values

Authenticity, creativity, and community. Every artwork on Artopia is 100% original and directly supports the artist who created it.
